# Privacy Policy for Project Planner

**Last Updated: October 1, 2025**

## Introduction

Project Planner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password, and organization details
- **Contact Information**: Phone numbers, addresses, and other contact details
- **Profile Information**: Job titles, roles, skills, and professional information
- **Project Data**: Project details, schedules, assignments, and related information

### Technical Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and app version
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app, features used, and time spent
- **Log Data**: IP addresses, timestamps, and error logs
- **Authentication Data**: Login credentials and session information

### Data You Provide
- **Project Information**: Project names, descriptions, timelines, and details
- **Team Data**: Operative information, manager details, and client information
- **Scheduling Data**: Bookings, assignments, and availability information
- **Communications**: Messages, notes, and other communications within the app

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Purposes
- **Service Provision**: To provide and maintain the Project Planner service
- **Account Management**: To create and manage user accounts and organizations
- **Project Management**: To facilitate project planning, scheduling, and tracking
- **Team Coordination**: To enable team collaboration and communication
- **Data Synchronization**: To sync data across devices and team members

### Secondary Purposes
- **Service Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
- **Customer Support**: To provide technical support and respond to inquiries
- **Security**: To protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
- **Compliance**: To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

#### Legal Requirements
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users

#### Business Transfers
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
- With your explicit consent

## Data Security

### Security Measures
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit and at rest

- **Authentication**: Secure user authentication and session management
- **Monitoring**: Continuous security monitoring and threat detection

### Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active

- **Deletion**: You can request data deletion at any time

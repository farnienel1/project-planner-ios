# Just Check the Box!

## What You're Seeing

Setting says: **"Turn Off SMTP auth only"**

## What to Do

**CHECK THE BOX** ✅

## Why This Is Confusing

Microsoft's wording is backwards:
- ☑ "Turn Off SMTP auth" CHECKED = SMTP is ENABLED ✅
- ☐ "Turn Off SMTP auth" UNCHECKED = SMTP is DISABLED ❌

It's asking: "Do you want to turn OFF the restriction?" = "Do you want to ENABLE SMTP?"

## Simple Rule

**Check it = Enable it** ✅

---

## After Checking

1. **Save** the setting
2. **Wait 10-15 minutes** for changes to propagate
3. **Restart your backend**
4. **Test email**

---

You're almost there! Just check that box and save! 🎉
